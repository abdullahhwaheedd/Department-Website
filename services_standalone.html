Our Services & Programs

The Computer Science Department offers a comprehensive range of programs and services designed to meet the diverse needs of our students and the broader academic community. From undergraduate education to advanced research opportunities, we provide pathways for success in the technology industry.

Academic Programs

Bachelor of Science in Computer Science

A comprehensive four-year program covering fundamental and advanced topics in computer science, including algorithms, data structures, software engineering, and artificial intelligence.

Master of Science in Computer Science

An advanced two-year program for students seeking specialized knowledge in areas such as machine learning, cybersecurity, distributed systems, and cloud computing.

Doctor of Philosophy in Computer Science

A research-focused doctoral program for students interested in conducting original research and contributing to the advancement of computer science knowledge.

Professional Certificates

Short-term specialized programs designed for professionals seeking to update their skills in emerging technologies such as cloud computing, AI, and cybersecurity.

Student Support Services

  • Academic Advising: Personalized guidance for course selection and career planning
  • Tutoring Services: One-on-one and group tutoring for challenging courses
  • Career Services: Resume review, interview preparation, and job placement assistance
  • Research Opportunities: Access to faculty-led research projects and internships
  • Mentorship Programs: Connections with industry professionals and alumni
  • Technical Support: Help with lab equipment, software, and computing resources
  • Scholarship Opportunities: Financial aid and merit-based scholarships
  • Student Organizations: Clubs and societies for networking and professional development

Facilities & Resources

  • Computer Labs: State-of-the-art laboratories with modern workstations and software
  • Research Centers: Dedicated spaces for AI, cybersecurity, and software engineering research
  • Library Resources: Extensive collection of textbooks, journals, and online databases
  • High-Performance Computing: Access to servers and clusters for computational research
  • Collaboration Spaces: Modern facilities for group projects and team-based learning
  • Seminar Rooms: Equipped spaces for lectures, workshops, and guest presentations
